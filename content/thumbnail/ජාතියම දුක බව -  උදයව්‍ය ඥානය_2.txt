[English]
Part 2 continues the jātiyam dukka discussion. The teacher
shows that the asti/nāsti division of hearing — "exists" when
sound is present, "doesn't exist" when absent — is built on
avijjā, since hearing itself never changed. This wrong
foundation (varaḍi padanama) IS mūla avijjā. Saṅkhāra (rāga
and dveṣa) arise directly from this division. Udayavyaya ñāṇa
is seeing this nittya foundation's wrongness — crossing from
nittya/sukha/attā to anicca/dukkha/anattā.
