[English]
Part 3 concludes the jātiyam dukka / udayavyaya ñāṇa series.
The teacher shows that both "hearing exists" and "hearing
doesn't exist" are mañññanā — conceptual constructions resting
on an ucchēda never actually experienced. Seeing this mañññanā
as mañññanā is seeing anicca. Once the padanama's wrongness
is seen, rāga/dveṣa cannot form: the asti/nāsti division
that generates them is dismantled. The path becomes nirāyāsa
— effortless — with nothing left to do or undo.
