[English]
A long, layered discussion dismantling every conceptual foothold
for understanding. Through the lamp analogy, ocean/rivers image,
and colour-perception exercises, the teacher shows that saññā
(recognition) was never actually performed — it is the marīci (mirage).
The culminating sleep/wakefulness exercise proves that neither
can be established: what remains is nodanna bava (not-knowing),
the very foundation from which all paṭiccasamuppāda arises.
When vidyā dawns, both seer and seen dissolve — like a tree that
lives without the concept of living.
