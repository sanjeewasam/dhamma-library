[English]
This discussion investigates maññanā (conceiving)
as the unseen structural foundation of experience.
Its real nature is "not-encounterability" — taking
what can never be met as though it were met. When
maññanā is seen as maññanā, memory, saṅkhāra
dukkha, and the sense of "I knew" all dissolve,
revealing why sabbe dhammā anattā uses "dhammā"
rather than "saṅkhārā" in the tilakkhaṇa.
