[English]
Discussion 04 explores why moha (delusion) is
structurally invisible — like bicycle balance,
never encountered as an object. Through analogies
of sewing-machine shuttles, colour recognition,
and fever thermometers, Mr. Lasantha shows that
vedanā and body mutually establish each other
with neither having independent ground, and
explains why saññā needs no separate anupassanā.
