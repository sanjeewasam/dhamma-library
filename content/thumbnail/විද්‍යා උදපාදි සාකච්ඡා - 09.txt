[English]
Discussion 09 examines why samudaya and nirodha
are not separate domains — seeing construction as
construction is itself nirodha. Through the matchbox
analogy, phassa is shown as structurally unfindable:
not in hetu, not in phala, not between them. Hearing
and seeing are analysed as diṭṭhi one is embedded in.
The journey's blank stretches reveal upekkhā as mūla
mōha. Nuvaṇa arises where viññāṇa cannot reach.
