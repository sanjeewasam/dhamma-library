[English]
A discussion on saṃsāra and nibbāna exploring
how understanding itself reconstitutes delusion.
Dhamma as medicine: both disease and cure vanish
together. The "aṭa" (eight) analogy shows that
what we "understand" has no findable root — the
frame is entirely self-constructed. Saṅkhāra dukkha
is meaning-making where meaning fundamentally
cannot be made.
