[English]
How saṃsāra became prescriptive: the false view
that sense organs produce perception has been
repeated across lifetimes until it functions as
truth. Sleep and dream analogies show waking
awareness is no more "real" than dreaming. Nibbāna
is not elsewhere — it is saṃsāra seen without
the error in view. Sammā diṭṭhi corrects
the seeing; nothing is gained or lost.
