About This Library

Objective

To make the profound vipassanā-style discussions of the late Mr Lasantha Manohara — and others who participated in them — more accessible to a wider audience.

Why This Archive Exists

The original recordings live on YouTube, where they must be streamed online and cannot be easily downloaded. This archive makes the recordings downloadable, and accompanies each one with a full Sinhala transcript and a detailed English summary — so that a person can read and reflect offline, on any device, at their own pace.
